Key Findings: Two Candidates Active on TV, Johnson Dominates Airwaves

As of February 13, 2026, Perry Johnson and Tom Leonard are the only declared gubernatorial candidates with FCC television filings. Johnson has a massive presence across both broadcast (8+ stations, 40+ files) and cable (8+ systems, 1,082+ files) spanning all major Michigan DMAs. Leonard has entered via cable systems in the Detroit and Flint-Saginaw markets under the label "AMP – TOM LEONARD FOR GOVERNOR" starting mid-January 2026, but has zero broadcast TV filings. No filings were found for John James, Mike Cox, Aric Nesbitt, or Ralph Rebandt in the 2026 governor race.
